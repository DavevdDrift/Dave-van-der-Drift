  • Digitization Klandizie Game

    Date: 24/06/2021
    Grade: 8.0

  • For my graduation project I digitized the Klandizie board game. The main goal of the game is to playfully create and increase Klandizie in the food retail sector. Klandizie consists of 5 competences. These are hospitality, product- and commodity knowledge, communication skills, empathy & passion, enthusiasm and drive.

    On the basis of knowledge-, drawing- and representation-assignments, awareness is playfully created. Because of this behavioral change will occur through knowledge of products, and joint discussions about situations that can occur in a company. It is an alternative to education, training and coaching.

    I worked from the New Designers company for an external client of the Vakcentrum company, which is part of the 3xM Food Foundation. Through research, conceptualization, design and testing, I finally made an app prototype of the Klandizie board game.

  • Issue

    The physical version of the Klandizie board game cannot be played, due to Corona. The game did not have a digital version, which could offer the same effectiveness and playful experience as the physical version. In addition, few physical copies have been released, making it difficult for companies to obtain a physical version.

  • Objective

    The goal is to digitize the board game, so that the target audience can easily and safely play the game during the Corona times. In doing so, it must be investigated which way of digitization gives the most effective product. The final product most stimulate a positive customer experience and gaming experience. In addition, it is necessary to find out what the most efficient way is to access and download the game.

  • Research Question

    A research question has been formulated based on the problem definition and the objective.

    Which method of digitization produces the most effective digital product, whereby a positive customer experience and playing experience is stimulated? Besides, what is the most efficient way to access and download the game?

  • Target Audience

    The primary target group are food-retailers and employers. The game has been made especially for this section and is geared towards it.

    The secondary target group are the 80% employees from the flexible layer and the 20% employees who have a career. The flexible layer consists of students who follow HAVO, HBO, VWO and WO.

  • Conclusion

    The User Needs from the research have served as inspiration for ideas about the concept. Preference was given to the idea of the multiplayer mode, where an assessor is present that judges the open answers. This idea was further developed with the help of the User Needs through a sitemap, wireframes, a lo-fi prototype and a hi-fi prototype. Feedback was collected by testing the hi-fi prototypes. The final hi-fi prototype is a digitization of the board game, in the form of an app.

  • Feedback

    Everyone was really happy and proud of the work that I did. They couldn’t wait to send the prototype to a developer in order to realise the application.
